Artificial Intelligence

We transform your data into value with tailored AI solutions. From academic research to industrial applications, we bring innovation to your business.

Artificial intelligence is the engine of modern innovation. At FORSEC, we develop AI solutions that emerge from university research and translate into concrete tools for businesses. Our approach combines scientific rigor with industrial pragmatism.

Process Mining

We extract knowledge from your system logs to discover how business processes actually work. We identify bottlenecks, deviations and optimization opportunities.

What's included:

  • Automatic process discovery from logs
  • Conformance checking against expected models
  • Performance and bottleneck analysis
  • Variant and deviation identification
  • Evidence-based optimization

AI for Cybersecurity

We develop machine learning solutions for anomaly detection, threat intelligence and proactive protection of IT and OT infrastructures.

What's included:

  • Anomaly detection for IT and OT networks
  • Automatic threat classification
  • Behavioral analysis for insider threats
  • SIEM and SOC integration
  • Custom models for your context

Security by design:

Our dual expertise in AI and cybersecurity enables us to develop solutions that not only use AI for security, but are secure by design, protecting sensitive data throughout the entire lifecycle.

Data Anonymization

We protect intellectual property and sensitive data while maintaining utility for analysis. Real-time solutions for Security Operations Centers and industrial environments.

What's included:

  • Real-time anonymization framework
  • Industrial intellectual property protection
  • Compatibility with anomaly detection systems
  • GDPR compliance and privacy by design
  • Controlled reversibility for incident response

AI Fast Prototyping

From your idea to proof-of-concept in rapid time. We validate AI solution feasibility before significant investments, reducing project risk.

What's included:

  • Feasibility analysis and requirements definition
  • Rapid Proof of Concept development
  • Validation on real data
  • Iteration toward MVP
  • Documentation and technology transfer

Agile approach:

Our iterative process enables rapid hypothesis validation and pivoting when necessary, minimizing time-to-market and the risk of developing solutions that don't meet real needs.

Custom AI Solutions

We develop custom algorithms and models for your specific needs. Not off-the-shelf solutions, but AI built on your domain and your data.

What's included:

  • Domain and available data analysis
  • Custom algorithm development
  • Training on proprietary data
  • Integration with existing systems
  • On-premise or cloud deployment

On-premise AI:

For those requiring complete control over their data, we offer AI solutions that run entirely in your infrastructure, without dependencies on external clouds.

Research & Innovation

We collaborate on funded research projects to bring the latest AI innovations to your company. Technology transfer from university to industry.

What's included:

  • Research grant collaboration
  • Technology transfer
  • Access to academic expertise
  • Co-development of innovative solutions
  • Joint publications

Academic network:

As a University of Calabria spin-off, we have direct access to DIMES expertise and collaborations with other Italian and European universities on PNRR and Horizon projects.
